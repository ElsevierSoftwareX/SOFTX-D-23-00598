void qocircuit::detector ( int  i_ch,
int  cond 
)

Adds a detector with a detection condition to a channel of the circuit. The readings in the rest of the channels are accepted only in the number of photons in this channel is equal to cond.

Parameters
inti_ch Detector channel.
intcond Detection condition. If cond -1 there is no condition and works as a normal detector.

Adds a general physical detector to a channel of the circuit.

Parameters
inti_ch Detector channel.
intcond Detection condition.
cond>=0: The readings in the rest of the channels are accepted only in the number of photons in this channel is equal to cond.
cond<0: There is no condition and works as a normal detector.
doubleeff Efficiency of the detector.
dubleblnk Ratio of time in which the detector is inactive due other detections.
doublegamma Average rate of dark counts in this channel.
