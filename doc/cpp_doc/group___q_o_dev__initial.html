Adds photons to the quantum optical device using a qubit definition in path encoding and their initial values.

Parameters
veciqinit qubit values
matiqmap Qubit definition. Matrix with a column entry for each qubit where they are defined the two photonic channels needed to encode the qubit. A 01 occupation means Q=0 and a 10 occupation Q=1.
